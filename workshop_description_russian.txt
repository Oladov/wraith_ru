Previously known as "Wraith Night: Single Lane" due to numerous update.

A survival game, inspired by the classic game, Wraith Night, that is design to be more playable when you are playing solo and shares same difficulty on playing with a partner or a team. The game is still on development and will have more rounds, items and further improvements in the future.

Goal : Survive and defend your towers with your friends.
Single Lane Map : 1 - 2 players
Triple Lane Map : 3 - 5 players
Current Final Round : 25 
Goal Final Round : 35
Version : 2.54
Dedicated Servers : Yes

All heroes and items included from the latest Dota 2 game are here. There are some slight changes to other heroes and items so that they are more playable for this custom game.
